About Me

Hi I'm Jordynn. I'm an UX designer from San Diego.

Growing up, I thought that I wanted to be a video editor. Other than my obsessive love for watching movies, this stemmed from my fine eye for details and the desire to create something to entertain others.

During college, I realized that my interests grew in other domains in the Arts such as visual design. By learning new skill sets, I could become a well rounded designer with varying abilities, instead of just concentrating on one single domain.

I want to use my skills and knowledge of design to make efficient and easy to use applications with a focus on design architecture.
